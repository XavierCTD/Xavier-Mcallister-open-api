Star Wars Character

Xavier Mcallister

Luke Skywalker

Luke Skywalker was raised on tatooine from two moisture farming couples. His Parents are senator Padme and jedi knight Anakin. He has defeated the dark lord Darth Vader by realizing that he is his son and convincing him that he can come back to the light side of the force. Through his training from a former jedi grandmaster named Yoda, the iconic hero will be known as one of the greatest jedi that has fought against the evil empire.
